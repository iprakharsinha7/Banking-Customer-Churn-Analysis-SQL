Business Insights & Recommendations

Key Findings

Churned customers held higher average balances than retained customers.
Inactive customers showed significantly higher churn rates.
Germany recorded the highest churn concentration among all regions.
High-value customers were also found among churned segments.

Strategic Recommendations
1. High-Value Customer Retention

Build premium retention programs for customers with high balances and strong banking history.

2. Inactivity Monitoring

Create early-warning engagement systems for inactive customers before churn occurs.

3. Region-Specific Strategies

Develop customized customer retention campaigns for high-risk markets like Germany.
